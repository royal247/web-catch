Brownies

This is my Favourite recipe.

List of Ingridients
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Nutella
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Pour flour inside a bowl
  2. Crack 3 eggs
  3. pour 250gm of nutelle into the bowl
  4. Mix it
  5. Pour the mixture into the baking pan
  6. Bake at 175 degrees centigrade for 15 minutes
  7. Allow to set and cool for 8 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
